Why voice

Most journaling apps ask you to write. Reflecsia asks you to talk. People speak about 4× faster than they type — a 5-minute voice entry captures what would take 18 minutes to write down. That’s why most journals get abandoned in week one. And why Reflecsia doesn’t.

What the AI actually does

Five quiet operations, running behind every entry.

  • Emotions
    Every entry mapped on Plutchik’s wheel + valence / arousal.
  • Themes
    Recurring topics surface across entries automatically.
  • Cognitive patterns
    Gentle CBT-aware reflection (not therapy, not a diagnosis).
  • Reflection prompts
    Questions personalised to what you actually said.
  • Summaries
    Daily, weekly, monthly. The longitudinal view.

Privacy & honesty

Audio is deleted right after the transcription completes — it never lives on our servers. Transcripts and analysis are encrypted at rest, scoped to your account by row-level security. We don’t sell data. We don’t train AI on your entries. You can export everything as PDF / Markdown / JSON and delete your account in one tap.

Reflecsia is a self-reflection tool, not a medical device. It helps you notice patterns. If something serious comes up, please talk to a licensed professional.
